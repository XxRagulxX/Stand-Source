=== License Keys ===

1. Create your account at https://stand.sh/account/register
2. Follow the instructions on the page (save your account id, etc.)
3. Once you're on the account dashboard, you can press "Download Launchpad." Your anti-virus might be causing you some trouble there.
4. Run the Launchpad. See https://stand.sh/help/troubleshooting if you're having trouble with that.
5. Ensure GTA V is running then inject Stand using the Launchpad.
6. Click on the activation key from the account dashboard to activate Stand.

=== Upgrade Keys ===

1. Go to https://stand.sh/account and ensure you're logged in
2. Scroll down until you see "Use your upgrade key"
3. Paste the upgrade key into the input box and press enter
